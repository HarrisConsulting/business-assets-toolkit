## ✅ Usage Notes

- Always check the license before using any asset in client deliverables
- Prefer assets with **Creative Commons Zero (CC0)** or **MIT** licenses for maximum flexibility
- Keep a local record of asset sources for attribution when required
- For paid client work, ensure commercial use is permitted
