## 🔤 Typography

### Primary Typeface
- **Font:** Inter (or substitute: Arial, Helvetica)
- **Source:** https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter
- **Use for:** Body copy, UI text, captions

### Display / Heading Typeface
- **Font:** Poppins
- **Source:** https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Poppins
- **Use for:** Headlines, titles, hero text

### Type Scale

| Style | Font | Size | Weight |
|-------|------|------|--------|
| H1 | Poppins | 36–48px | 700 (Bold) |
| H2 | Poppins | 28–36px | 600 (SemiBold) |
| H3 | Poppins | 22–28px | 600 (SemiBold) |
| Body | Inter | 16px | 400 (Regular) |
| Small | Inter | 14px | 400 (Regular) |
| Caption | Inter | 12px | 400 (Regular) |

## 🖼️ Logo Usage

### Approved Variations
- **Primary Logo:** Full color on light backgrounds
- **Reversed Logo:** White/light version for dark backgrounds
- **Icon Mark:** Standalone icon for favicons, app icons, small formats

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the logo's "H" letterform on all sides.

### Minimum Sizes
- Print: 1 inch / 25mm wide minimum
- Digital: 120px wide minimum

### Logo Don'ts
- ❌ Do not stretch or distort the logo
- ❌ Do not change logo colors outside approved palette
- ❌ Do not place on a busy background without a clear container
- ❌ Do not add shadows or effects
- ❌ Do not rotate the logo

---

## 📸 Imagery Style

- **Photography tone:** Professional, clean, and authentic — real people in real work environments
- **Avoid:** Generic stock imagery that feels staged or impersonal
- **Preferred sources:** Unsplash, Pexels (see [design-resources.md](../design-resources.md))
- **Image treatment:** Natural color grading; avoid heavy filters
